Github · Google Scholar · Linkedin

I am a third year PhD student within the Biomedical and Astronomical Signal Processing (BASP) laboratory at Heriot-Watt University, Edinburgh, UK. I am fortunate to be working under the supervision of Prof. Yves Wiaux and Prof. Jean-Christophe Pesquet. My research mainly focuses on designing optimisation algorithms for solving imaging problems, with applications in radio-interferometry and biomedical imaging. Feel free to reach me by email :)
